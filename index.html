Common Questions

Honest answers

We believe in transparency. Here's what most clients ask before they sign on.

Do I need to understand AI to work with you? +
Not at all. Our job is to handle the complexity so you don't have to. We explain everything in plain language and focus on business outcomes, not technology.
How long until I see results? +
Most clients see measurable time savings within the first 4–8 weeks. Your first AI workflow is typically live within 2–6 weeks of the project starting.
Will this integrate with our existing software? +
Yes. We design every solution to work with your existing stack — Xero, MYOB, Simpro, Procore, Microsoft 365, and hundreds of other platforms. We don't force you to change what's working.
Is there a lock-in contract? +
No. Our retainer agreements are month-to-month. We believe we should earn your business every month. Project work is quoted and agreed upfront with clear deliverables.
What happens to our data? +
Your data stays yours. We follow Australian Privacy Act requirements and only use your data to build and improve your specific solution. We never train AI models on your data without explicit permission.
Do you work with businesses outside Brisbane? +
Absolutely. We work with clients across Australia. Most of our work is delivered remotely, and we can travel for on-site workshops and implementation when needed.
